Faith Heritage Family (FHF) a Grassroots Humanitarian Organization, based in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. The group was founded in 2010 as a vehicle to reach out to the very poor indigenous residents of the Water front (Waterside) communities in Port Harcourt.
Her Mission is to Inspire the Despairing to Aspire. Scope of work; Since 2010, she has been organizing workshops on Sensitization/ Awareness on how to manage/ tackle the spread of HIV/AIDS in these vulnerable communities.
Her Vision is to reduce HIV/AIDS and extreme poverty to the barest minimum. Her catchment areas are the water front communities, locally known as “Water side” settlements.
